Shield Insurance

Dashboard Video Presentation

Overview

Shield Insurance Project -- Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to explore customer behavior and revenue patterns. Analyzed trends across age groups, time periods, and sales channels. Delivered insights to support data-driven decision-making in the insurance domain.

Tools Used

  • Power BI

Problem statement

    Shield Insurance aims to uncover actionable insights from customer and sales data to guide policy improvements and strategic decisions. By analyzing trends across age groups, sales channels, and time periods, the company seeks to upgrade its offerings and align them with customer behavior — ultimately driving higher revenue and better market positioning.

Task

    To design an interactive Power BI dashboard for Shield Insurance that analyzes customer distribution, revenue patterns, and sales mode performance across different time periods. The goal was to extract meaningful insights that could help improve business decisions and policy offerings.

Action

  • Collected and explored customer and revenue data.
  • Built interactive dashboards with KPIs, charts, and slicers.
  • Analyzed the trend of customer and revenue by age group, month, and sales mode.
  • Derived actionable insights to support policy upgrades and revenue growth.

Results

  • Identified a significant surge in customer acquisition during March, linked to end-of-year tax-saving behavior.
  • Discovered a moderate rise in customer count in December due to early tax planning and bonuses.
  • Found that the 65+ age group, while only 8% of customers, contributed 20% of total revenue — highlighting a high-value segment.
  • Observed a shift from offline to online sales channels, indicating changing customer preferences.
  • Detected balanced performance in some sales modes, contributing equally to revenue and customer count.
  • Gained insights to help optimize sales strategy and support data-driven policy improvements.
